Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to delete your ScanSync account and what happens to your data.

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account profile and settings
  • All scanned documents and images
  • Folders and organizational data
  • Workflow configurations
  • Credit balance and transaction history
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please export any scans you want to keep before requesting deletion.

How to Request Deletion

Option 1: From the App

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account and follow the prompts.

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to our support team with the subject line "Account Deletion Request":

Request Deletion via Email

Please include the email address associated with your Google Sign-In.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

Data Retained

After deletion, we may retain:

  • Anonymized analytics data (cannot identify you)
  • Records required for legal/financial compliance

Third-Party Services

Deleting your ScanSync account does not affect:

  • Your Google account
  • Files you exported to Google Drive or OneDrive
  • Your Google Play purchase history

To revoke ScanSync's access to these services, visit your Google Account permissions or Microsoft Account permissions.

Questions?

If you have questions about account deletion or data privacy, contact us at support@scan-sync.com.
